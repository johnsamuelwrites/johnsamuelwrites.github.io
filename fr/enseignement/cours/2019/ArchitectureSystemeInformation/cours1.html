4. Towards Federated Search

Federated Search

  • No centralized strore for all data
  • Multiple (autonomous) search engines respond to search queries
  • Every search engine has its own crawling, indexing and
  • Centralized approach for broadcasting queries
  • Centralized approach for processing responses by the search engines
