About Me

John Samuel

Enseignant-Chercheur in Urban Data Science & Knowledge Graphs

I teach topics related to the web, programming, and data science. My current research works are on (3D) urban data analysis and knowledge graphs. I love reading, photography, programming, watching series or documentaries, and experimenting with open-source solutions during my free time. I am also the creator of Multilingual - an AI-driven programming language that transcends any single human language.

Pronouns: he/they
View Detailed CV

I also contribute to Wikimedia projects, especially Wikidata and Wikimedia Commons. Sometimes, I turn some of my experimental scripts into tools for the Wikidata community. I also share relevant articles on open science, open-source, open education, and (multilingual) open data on social media.
