Prolog

  • Declarative programming language
  • Logic programming language: based on first-order logic
  • Developed in 1972 by Alain Colmerauer
  • Expressed in terms of relations: facts and rules
  • Used for theorem proving, expert systems and NLP

Prolog: Rules

A Prolog program contains clauses of the following form.

Head :- Body.
  • Body may contain one or more predicates using conjunction and disjunction
  • Head does not contain any conjunction and disjunction
