Acknowledgements

Rediscovering Life Memories with SVG and Inkscape

John Samuel

Gratitude and Recognition

This project would not have been possible without the support, encouragement, and valuable feedback from many individuals who have accompanied me on this artistic journey. I am deeply grateful to all who have contributed to the development and evolution of Project Drawing.

Friends and Reviewers

To all my friends who have provided valuable comments and constructive feedback throughout the course of this project—your insights have been instrumental in shaping my artistic direction. Your honest observations, suggestions for improvement, and words of encouragement have helped me refine my approach and maintain momentum during challenging periods.

Your willingness to engage with these works, to see beyond their simplicity, and to offer thoughtful critique has made this project richer and more meaningful. Each conversation, each suggestion, and each moment of shared appreciation has contributed to my growth as an artist.

Open Source Community

I extend my sincere appreciation to the Inkscape development community and all contributors to this remarkable open-source software. Inkscape has been the foundation of this entire project, providing powerful tools that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their resources. The dedication of the Inkscape team to creating and maintaining free, professional-grade vector graphics software has made projects like mine possible.

The broader open-source community deserves recognition for fostering an environment where knowledge is shared freely, tools are developed collaboratively, and creativity is democratized. This project stands on the shoulders of countless contributors who believe in the power of open technology and collaborative development.

Sources of Inspiration

I am grateful to all the artists, educators, and writers whose work has inspired my exploration of vector graphics and digital art. The resources, tutorials, and documentation available online have been invaluable learning tools throughout this journey.

Special thanks to those who have shared their techniques, insights, and experiences in working with SVG and vector graphics. Your generosity in sharing knowledge has accelerated my learning and opened new creative possibilities.

To everyone who has viewed, shared, or discussed these drawings—thank you for being part of this retrospective journey. Your engagement transforms these simple vector graphics from personal memories into shared experiences, and for that, I am truly grateful.
