Books on Color and Perspective

These foundational texts have shaped my understanding of color theory, visual perception, and spatial representation‚Äîall essential elements in creating effective chromatic art.

    A comprehensive exploration of color theory and practice, examining how color affects perception, emotion, and artistic expression. Edwards' accessible approach demystifies color relationships and provides practical guidance for developing chromatic sensitivity.

  • Perspective Made Easy Ernest R. Norling

Online Resources and Documentation

Digital resources have been instrumental in learning specific technical skills and discovering new chromatic techniques.
