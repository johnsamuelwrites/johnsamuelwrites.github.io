The Long Wait

A poem by John Samuel

I am ready to wait.
I am ready to wait,
Till you give me back
My dignity and my voice.
The wait is long,
But I am patient.

A lot of us look at you,
With supressed voices.
I do the same,
Hoping that one day,
You will change your mind
And give us back
Our dignity and our voice.
